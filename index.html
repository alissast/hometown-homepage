Visit Santa Rosa, California

Enjoy colorful, varied local cuisine, rolling hills speckled with oaks, or venture west to find yourself awestruck amongst towering redwoods or at the edge of windswept cliffs facing the sea.

Top three things to do when you visit Santa Rosa

Stroll through Luther Burbank Home and Gardens

Take in Victorian-era charm during a morning stroll through the gardens, greenhouse, and home of nioted American horticulturist Luther Burbank (1849-1926).

Enjoy Historic Railroad Square

Enjoy the local food scene and shop for antiques, find gorgeous yarn for your next knitting or crochet project, or board the SMART train and take a ride through the charming countryside.

Take a day trip to the coast and the redwoods

Venture out west to bask in the glory of the towering redwoods at Armstrong Redwoods State Natural Reserve, followed by a moment of rest at Goat Rock Beach in Jenner.
